Hozho

Two best friends, Celia and Cathy make the move from Wellington to Auckland, excited about a fresh start at university. Cathy leaves behind a broken heart and Celia the countless teens she's more than happy to forget. During the first night at their new hostel they meet Lloyd, the introverted movie geek looking to broaden his horizons. Hozho chronicles these three unlikely teens who learn the power of friendship when they find themselves at the centre of the world's attention.

All issues written & lettered by Kenneth Chapman and penciled & inked by Nathan Foon.
