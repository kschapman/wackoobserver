The Intrigue

The Intrigue is an interview podcast hosted by Kenneth Chapman where everything from childhood dreams, religious beliefs and sexuality are discussed over hours of unedited conversation.

The Intrigue Episode 9 | Tai Berdinner-Blades

Tai is a film, television and theatre actress who shares her upbringing and experiences at high school and drama school before getting into her work as a season 5 regular on Go Girls to now featuring in Tatau, a BBC series set in the Cook Islands.

Recorded on July 10th 2015 - Download the Mp3

The Intrigue Episode 8 | Olivia Poppe

The latest guest on the podcast is the beautiful and hilarious Olivia Poppe who opens with an entertaining recount of her experience as an extra on the Hobbit before getting into her story of becoming a model and her ambitions for a career in film.

Recorded on June 5th 2015 - Download the Mp3

The Intrigue Episode 7 | Keeley O'Hagan

Keeley O’Hagan recently won the national women’s title for high jump in New Zealand and was kind enough to come on the podcast to talk about her career so far and her goals for the future where I know she’ll be going a long way :)

Recorded on May 13th 2015 - Download the Mp3

The Intrigue Episode 6 | Anna Hicks

I knew of Anna as a local designer in Wellington so after meeting her at an overlocking class she runs through her work, I thought she’d be a perfect guest for the show. Anna describes her childhood where she started making clothes and patterns at a really young age, right through to designing her graduate collection at Massey University and what she’s working on now.

The Intrigue Episode 5 | Holly Bazett

Holly Bazett is an artist currently residing in Sussex, England. I was able to meet her through the tennis club we both used to belong to in Wellington when she lived in NZ and after hearing she was an artist and only talking for a few minutes she was kind enough to agree to come on the podcast. She describes a challenging childhood, her travels which include living in Botswana for two years and having her first child there to studying at university later in life and now painting everyday.

Recorded on May 1st 2015 - Download the Mp3

www.hollybazett.com

The Intrigue Episode 4 | Lauren Mann

Lauren Mann is the current editor of Wellington Woman magazine and also a former Miss Wellington. She tells us about growing up in Thailand and the UK before settling down in New Zealand; shares her experiences competing in pageant shows; explains her love for surf life saving; discusses her thoughts on rape culture and much more.

Recorded Monday 13th April 2015 - Download the Mp3

The Intrigue Episode 1 | Bex Brent - Part 1

Bex currently owns and operates the Willis York hair salon. I had the pleasure of meeting her at the salon while doing some filming there. After talking to her for no more than a couple minutes before having to leave, I felt, as I do with some of the people I meet every now and then, that I just had to hear her story. Something about her really struck something in me and she kindly agreed to be the first guest on the show. In part one of her story, Bex takes us through her experiences growing up down south, school girl crushes, life at boarding school and falling in love with music.
